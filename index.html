Why Horse?

Horse is a very unique animal, and belongs to the family Equidae. Horses are know for their strength, speed, and their abilities to be trained for varities of tasks(including riding etc.)

Types of Horse

A horse type is not related to breed but rather by their uses. Some might be selected for breeding, work load or other functions. Types include;

  • Grade horse, a horse of unknowned or mixed breed perentage
  • Hunter, a type of jumping horse, either a show hunter or a field hunter
  • Draft horse
  • Sport horse, includes any breeds suitable for use in assorted international competitive disciplines governed by the FEI.
